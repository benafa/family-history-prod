    Launch Day!

    Today - June 16 2023 - is the official launch of colombochetty.com!

    With over 200 historical documents and 1200+ people in our database, colombochetty.com aims to preserve the genealogy of the Colombo Chetty people.

    A quick aside… June 16 also happens to be the birthday of my grandfather, Benson Anandappa, as well as his brothers Tony and Regis - they were coincidentally born on the same day in different years! I would like to dedicate this website to these three brothers. While Benson and Regis have passed, Tony is still alive and well - happy 98th birthday!!

    Check out this view overvew of the site:

    Thank you to everyone who has taken the time to talk with me and share your memories. I’ve had so much fun creating working on this project and I’m so happy that to now share it with all of you through this website.

    This website is an on-going project and I plan to continually add content and create posts. Here are a few features that I would love to add in the future

    • Calendar of birth, death and marriage dates of ancestors (and the living!)
    • Map of the current location of Colombo Chetties
    • A recreation of New Chetty Street from the early 1900s
    • More detailed stories of ancestors

    Please email ben@colombochetty.com with any questions or comments.

    – Ben Anandappa
